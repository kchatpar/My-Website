Krishna Chatpar


My name is Krishna Chatpar, I was born in India and came to the U.S. when I was young.
I grew up in Dix Hills, New York and graduated from Half Hollow Hills High School West.
I recently graduated from the University at Buffalo with a B.A. in Computer Science and a concentration in Biology.
My favorite programming languages to code in are: Python, Java, and R.
Some of my favorite frameworks are Spark and Hadoop.
And I'm always looking for new languages and packages to learn.
A fun fact: My first language was actually French.
I speak English and French fluently and I'm currently learning Spanish.
